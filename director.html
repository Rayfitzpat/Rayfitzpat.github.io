Planets and Activities of Destiny 2

Titan

A methane ocean moon. The Hive and the Fallen lurk around the moon.

Earth's Moon

Earth's only natural satellite. Once the site of a small colony for supplying the rest of the system,
now the location of the Hive's stronghold, with a minor House of Exile presence.

IO

A volcanic, sulfuric moon occupied by the Cabal and Vex.
Vex ruins lie all across the planet, and the Taken are highly active.

Earth

Mankind's homeworld. The only safe human settlement is The Last City, with pockets of hidden human communities. The Fallen House of Devils and House of Kings control the rest of the planet.

Mars

The first human city outside of Earth, Freehold, was founded here.
Now occupied by the Cabal, who are fighting invading Vex forces for control.

Mercury

A former garden world, now converted by the Vex into a planet-sized machine.

Nessus

A dormant Vex Machine World that is also under Red Legion occupation.

The Tower

The Tower is a social space located atop the defensive wall encircling the Last City.
It serves as the headquarters of the Vanguard and the home of the Guardians.

The Dreaming City

It is the most sacred location of the Reefborn Awoken, and is where they study and meditate on greater
paracausal powers through dreaming.

The Tangled Shore

The Tangled Shore is an asteroid wasteland that lays within The Reef.
The leadership of the region is deligated to The Spider and his outsider Fallen faction.

Vanguard Strikes

Strikes are a PvE activity constructed for a fireteam of one to three players. Strikes are similar to missions in that they feature a series of objectives culminating in a battle against an Ultra, or boss. There are currently 8 available Strikes, each with its own objectives, enemies, and rewards.

Gambit

Gambit is a multiplayer game type introduced in Destiny 2: Forsaken. It is run by The Drifter, a rogue Guardian. This mode is a hybrid PVP/PVE mode that features two teams of four as they compete across three rounds.

Crucible

The Crucible is a competitive player-vs-player (PvP) multiplayer activity in Destiny 2. Crucible includes two ranking systems: Valor and Glory.
